About Me

I'm a Junior Biomedical Sciences Major with a minor in Chemistry. Chemistry is a great passion of mine and I could only hope that I can help you like it the way I do! I decided to start tutoring when I helped several of my friends in their Intro Chemistry courses and they benefited greatly from my help.

I have been conducting research in the Chemistry Department for a full year now, and hope to publish a paper soon for the undergraduate synthesis of a large organic molecule called Indene-fused Quincridone

I'm easy to get along with and have the knowhow to break down the more complicated Intro Chemistry subjects to where they are easy to understand and (maybe) even fun to do!
